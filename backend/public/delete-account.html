Delete Your Squad App Account

This page allows you to request the permanent deletion of your Squad App account and all associated data, as required by the Google Play Store policy.

Warning: This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all your data, including your profile, posts, comments, and chats, will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

To proceed with account deletion, you must initiate the request from within the Squad App:

  1. Open the Squad App on your device.
  2. Navigate to your **Profile** screen.
  3. Tap on the **Settings** icon.
  4. Select the **"Delete Account"** option.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion. You will be required to enter your password to finalize the request.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon successful deletion, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your personal profile information (name, email, bio, profile photos).
  • All posts, comments, and reactions you have created.
  • Your follower and following relationships.
  • All chat messages and media shared.
  • Your notification and activity history.

Data Retention

For legal and security purposes, some anonymized, non-personal data may be retained. All personally identifiable information will be deleted within **30 days** of your request, in compliance with our data retention policy.

Need Help?

If you have trouble deleting your account through the app or have any questions, please contact our support team at support@squad.com.
